∑x=0⌊ca⌋(⌊c−axb⌋+1)\sum_{x=0}^{\left\lfloor\frac{c}{a}\right\rfloor}\left(\left\lfloor\frac{c-ax}{b}\right\rfloor+1\right) x=0∑⌊ac​⌋​(⌊bc−ax​⌋+1)
